About Me

She/Her/Hers

I am currently a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow and previous Clare Boothe Luce Fellow and at Boston University. I am a member of The Perkins INfrared Exosatellite Survey collaboration, and study the physical mechanisms behind the variability of L and T type dwarfs. I am passionate about accesibility to STEM fields, and focus my outreach efforts on inclusivity of minority groups into physics and astronomy.

Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, baking, and playing with my dog, Tesla!
