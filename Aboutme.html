About Me

She/Her/Hers

I am currently a Clare Boothe Luce Fellow and National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow at Boston University. I am a member of The Perkins INfrared Exosatellite Survey collaboration, and study the physical mechanisms behind the variability of L and T type dwarfs. I am extremely passionate about accesibility to STEM fields, and focus my Outreach efforts on inclusivity of minority groups into physics and astronomy.

Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, baking, and playing with my dog, Tesla!
